Rules

  • Always submit your team two hours before each match day.
  • You can only choose 15 players for each matchday.
  • A player red carded will result in 10 points deducted from your score while yellow result in 5 points deducted.
  • A player that score hat-trick(3 goals in a match) will give you 50 points.
  • A goal scored by a player in your team gives 15 points.
  • A save by your keeper will result in 5 points added to your score.
  • Away goals scored has a score of 25 points.
  • Maintaining a team for 4 consecutive matchdays will give a score of five points.
